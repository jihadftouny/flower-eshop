About ABZ Flowers
Welcome to ABZ Flowers, your premier flower shop in Beirut, Haret Hreik. We are a family-owned business with a deep-rooted passion for flowers and a rich history in the floral industry. Our story begins with our founder, who inherited his love for flowers from his father, a florist who dedicated his entire life to creating stunning floral arrangements.

Originally, our owner had a flower shop located in another vibrant area of Beirut, Ras el Nabaa. However, driven by a desire to build a better future for his family, he made the difficult decision to leave Lebanon. After years of hard work and perseverance abroad, he returned to his beloved homeland in 2017 to pursue his lifelong dream of working as a florist.

We take great pride in sourcing the freshest and most vibrant flowers from local growers and international suppliers to ensure the highest quality for our customers. Whether you're looking for a breathtaking bouquet for a special occasion, elegant floral centerpieces for weddings and events, or a simple gesture of love and appreciation, our floral artisans will design and arrange the perfect ensemble to exceed your expectations.

Beyond our commitment to delivering exceptional floral designs, we also strive to provide personalized service that leaves a lasting impression. We take the time to understand your preferences, offer expert advice, and ensure that every detail is tailored to your specific needs.

As a local flower shop, we feel deeply connected to our community in Beirut. We embrace the beauty and resilience of our city, and our mission is to spread joy and positivity through the language of flowers. We are honored to be part of life's most meaningful moments, from birthdays and anniversaries to celebrations and condolences.

Visit ABZ Flowers in Haret Hreik, and immerse yourself in a world of floral elegance, simplicity and enchantment!
